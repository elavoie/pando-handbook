usage: hyperparams [OPTIONS]

Returns hyperparameters for the rldemo as line-delimited JSON objects.

Options (Learning Rate):

--low=L   (L=0.001, Number) 
          Lower-bound of the range, inclusive.

--high=H  (H=0.001, Number) 
          Higher-bound of the range, inclusive.

--steps=S (S=1, Number)
          Number of steps to output.
